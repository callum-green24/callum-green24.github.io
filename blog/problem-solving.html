Problem Solving

When was I blocked on a simple problem?

Now to me this wasn’t a simple problem. Still isn’t, if I was asked to do it again now without internet I’m not confident I could.
We were asked to find the first object Scooby was hiding in using a function and the .find() method. I began with pseudocode but did not find that helpful so I jumped into searching the internet for solutions. At this point I had been doing the Kata for awhile and didn’t feel like I was taking anything in as well as getting frustrated so I ended up taking a break. When I got back it was back to reading and from there a bunch of trial and error until I eventually got it right. What I learned is sometimes rest is the best path to a solution.

When did I elegantly solving a problem?

I wouldn’t say it was elegant but I was happy with the way I figured out how to get an average out of an array because ,without googling anything I was very close to getting my ‘for loop’ correct on the first try. What I got wrong I was able to figure out very quickly searching for solutions online. I learned that I am picking things up even if it feels like Im 10 steps behind.

Confidence in problem solving techniques

  • Pseudocode: Needs a lot of work but I see how useful it could be once I get the hang of it
  • Try something/Googling:This combination is my go to currently although I would like to be less reliant on google
  • Rubber ducky method: I do this a lot and find it helpful. I don’t have a rubber duck.
  • Console.logging:I don’t use this nearly enough and am not confident with it.
  • Asking your peers/coaches for help:Not something I did nearly enough but I wouldn’t say its an issue with confidence.
  • Reflection:I feel like I’m constantly reflecting in my head but I need to improve on being intentional with it and writing it in some form or another.

Reflections on being reluctant to ask for help

I could have and should have asked for help on a lot of the Kata, I ended up exceeding the time box by a fair bit. It stems from a mix of things. Im stubborn and believe I can figure things out given time, I don’t want to waste other peoples time and I don’t want people to realise how bad at this I am. What can I do differently? The opposite of what I was doing this kata.
